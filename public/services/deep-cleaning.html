What is Deep Cleaning?

Deep cleaning goes beyond regular maintenance cleaning. It's an intensive, thorough clean that tackles areas often overlooked in day-to-day cleaning—high-level dusting, detailed sanitisation, grout cleaning, and restoration of surfaces to their original condition.

Whether you're preparing for a special event, recovering from building works, or simply need to reset your space, a deep clean provides the fresh start your environment needs.

When You Need a Deep Clean

  • Before moving into new premises – Start fresh in a truly clean environment
  • After refurbishment or building works – Remove construction dust and debris
  • Periodic refresh – Annual or quarterly intensive cleans to maintain standards
  • End of tenancy – Prepare spaces for new occupants
  • Pre-inspection preparation – Get ready for audits or important visits

What's Included

Our deep cleaning service is customised to your specific requirements, but typically includes:

  • High-level dusting (vents, light fittings, tops of cabinets)
  • Detailed cleaning of all surfaces
  • Thorough sanitisation of touchpoints
  • Interior window cleaning
  • Floor scrubbing and restoration
  • Kitchen and break room deep clean
  • Washroom deep sanitisation
  • Carpet extraction or hard floor treatment

Our Approach

Every deep clean begins with a site assessment. We'll walk through your space, identify priority areas, and create a detailed scope of work. You'll know exactly what's being done, when, and by whom.

Our teams work methodically—top to bottom, room by room—ensuring nothing is missed. We use commercial-grade equipment and professional cleaning products appropriate for each surface type.
