Cleaning Services Tailored to Your Business

Every commercial space has different demands. A busy medical practice needs a very different cleaning approach to a corporate office or an industrial warehouse. That is why we do not offer one-size-fits-all packages. Instead, we work with you to build a cleaning programme that matches your site, your schedule, and your standards.

Our services fall into three core categories, each designed to address a specific need. Whether you require regular day-to-day maintenance, a thorough periodic deep clean, or specialist work that calls for specific equipment and expertise, we have the team and the systems to deliver.

How We Deliver

What sets Signature Cleans apart is the way we operate. Every service we provide is backed by documented cleaning specifications, dedicated teams assigned to your site, and regular quality audits shared directly with you. This systems-driven approach means you get consistent results, full transparency, and a single point of contact for everything.

We are SSIP accredited and CQMS verified, meeting the compliance standards that facilities managers and procurement teams expect. From small offices to multi-site operations across Devon, Cornwall, and Somerset, we scale our services to fit.
