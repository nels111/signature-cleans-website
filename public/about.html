Our Story

Signature Cleans was founded in 2022 by Nick Stentiford with a simple observation: too many cleaning companies were inconsistent, unreliable, and reactive.

Starting with holiday let management, we quickly discovered that reliable, quality cleaning was the cornerstone of excellent service. When local businesses began asking for the same standard, Signature Cleans evolved into what it is today – a commercial cleaning company built on documented systems, proactive communication, and genuine accountability.

Today, we serve offices, medical practices, car showrooms, care homes, and residential blocks across Devon, Cornwall, and Somerset. Our goal isn't to be the biggest – it's to be the most consistent.
